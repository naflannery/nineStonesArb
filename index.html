About

Nine Stones Services is a Dorset based Aboricultaural and garden maintainance company headed by Gwynfor Ashford.

Located in an area of outstanding natural beauty we pride ourselves high standards of work deserving of our wonderfull landscape.

We are City and Guilds NPTC Certified

We are happy to take on all manner of tasks but for a full list of services provided please see below.

Services

All our services are competitively priced. For a quote or to arrange a site visit to discuss works please get in touch.

Services we offer are:

  • tree care
  • hedge trimming
  • bramble / scrub clearance
  • grass cutting
  • chainsaw grounds maintenance
